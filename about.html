What is Dreams Givers? About Our Mission

Dear Friends,

We are a group of young adults from around the world who have come together to create educational opportunities to the young children of Laos. We collectively support and guide children to pursue their dreams and discover their potentials to lift themselves out of their cycle of poverty. We offer hopes and opportunities to under-privileged children of Laos by supporting them on their educational path, allowing them the opportunity to see the larger world.

We kindly ask for your support to help us provide opportunities for children in Laos especially the ones who are in the rural areas, who are not privileged to attend school or educational curriculum.
